Effective Date: June 5, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help improve your experience and maintain session integrity.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Cookies that remain on your device until expiration or deletion.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies solely for security, session management, and performance enhancement. We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking your behavior beyond what is necessary.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note disabling some cookies may affect your user experience.

5. More Information

For questions, contact us at cookies@deepelynx.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will appear here.

Deepelynx respects your choices and protects your data integrity.
