The Access Grant

Three exclusive tiers. One opportunity at each. And a path that grows as you share it.

1. Your Entry, Your Branch

Upon registration, you receive a unique access code and a personal invitation link. These are sent directly to your inbox with a warm welcome. Think of it as your branch on the Deepelynx tree.

2. Grow Your Line. Multiply Your Opportunities.

Every person who joins through your invitation link grants you +1 additional access opportunity. Invite two? You now hold 3 chances: one for each exclusive tier—Pro, Business, and Ultimate.

Note: Your invitees (like B and C) cannot circle back to you or each other. The tree never loops back. Each new invite extends the branch forward.

3. Purchase Additional Opportunities

Want to increase your chances further? You may acquire extra access opportunities. These function like exclusive tickets—more chances, though never guaranteed.

You decide how to allocate them: all toward the Pro tier? Divided between Business and Ultimate? The strategy is yours.

4. Grant Ceremonies & Distribution

The access grants proceed in three exclusive phases:

  • Pro Ceremony – Lifetime access to all Pro tools
  • Business Ceremony – Lifetime access including team features
  • Ultimate Ceremony – Lifetime access to everything Deepelynx will become

Each ceremony selects from entries valid for that tier. Recipients are notified via email and granted immediate access.

5. Upgrades & Unlocks

Secured a Pro tier and wish for more? You may upgrade permanently by paying the one-time annual price of the next tier. Afterwards, no recurring fees.

You ascend once — the summit is yours to keep.

Fair. Transparent. Branch by branch.
